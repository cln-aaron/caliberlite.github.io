Why This Assessment?

The Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) programme covers 20 distinct security domains — from network scanning and malware analysis to cloud security and cryptography. Students arrive with vastly different backgrounds. This pre-course assessment is designed to establish your baseline across all CEH domains, identify specific areas where you may need additional preparation, and help your instructor tailor the course experience to maximise your learning outcome. Your honest responses ensure the most accurate diagnostic.
